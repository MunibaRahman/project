What should you do?

To play the farm game complete the following tasks:
1. Use your arrow keys to find the coin in the maze
2. Ride the tractor to find and mow the overgrown grass scattered around the farm.
3. Train for the horse race by avoiding the obstacles and collecting the trophy.
4. Collect eggs in the barn but avoid the angry chickens using your arrow keys
